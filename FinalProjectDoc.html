Final project documentation

The structural integrity of old furniture may be the main reason why it is discarded. Replacement of structural components with 3D printed parts is a common solution. Topology optimized 3D printed parts can provide the same functionality with less material.

Image of chair I will be using. It is very worn down and was in my parents backyard made of metal. I will be cutting off some parts of the chair and replacing them with 3D printed parts.

I created a general box with measurements of the part I want to replace.

I Populated random points within this box. This is parameterized and can change the number of points with the slider. You can also change the seed, which gives another set of random points. Using Voronoi3D, I divided the box based on the points. Voronoi3D divides the region based on all the points close to the seed which is the first random point I generated.
