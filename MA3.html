MA2: “A seat at the Table”

This mini assignment is aimed to give experience going through a 3D Printing CAD/CAM workflow. We will computationally generate a parameterized design using Grasshopper/Python in Rhinoceros 3D.

Task 1: Create a Parametric Table or Chair Design

I modeled a sphere and molded its shape to look like a seat. I used its geometry on Grasshopper and was able to slice the basic shape. Sliders can change the space between each slice and the direction its sliced. The two verions of the model help show some of the parameters. The following image is the grasshopper file.

Task 2: Fabricate Your Design With 3D Printing

I decided to use the second design to print. I decided to download the Prusa slicer and check how the print would come and to verify it works. The total print was about an hour and a half.
