"Go-Kart" was created using SketchUp. This kart was designed as a "possible" addition to Mario Kart.

These refrences and sketches were the first steps to creating the design on SketchUp. SketchUp isn't the best tool fo curves and natural shapes, which resulted in a difficult process to create the design. This project also helped devlope skills in setting up different perspectives and images of the design.

The design features the kart with a green, yellow, red, and black livery, that includes the nuber 10 and "Form". The design also showcases a parachute with a matching pattern.
