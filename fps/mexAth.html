"Mexican Athletes" attempts to bring recongition to sucessful Mexican athletes. The initial idea was to inspire the next generation of athletes.

The designs featured a wide range of sucessful athletes. At times its hard to relate to something or someone, but having inspiration from somone that looks like you or is from the same country can help push someone to accomplish their dreams.

The posters were made using Procreate and Adobe Photoshop.
