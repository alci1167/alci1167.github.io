DAP-P3R

Idea to clean and upkeep the Denver Union Station. To solve this problem in the true style of speculative design, by fiction and imagination, we decided to build off of the well-known automatic household vacuum: the Roomba. Responsible for both vacuuming and standing as a reminder to throw away trash, DAP-P3R is guaranteed to keep Union Station consistently looking dapper

Mexican Athletes

This project puts a spotlight on Mexican athletes. This collection of posters, helps show off some of the top Mexican athletes in different sports around the world. They were created using Adobe Illustrator. The designs have some influences from new wave, bauhaus, and digital art.
