006 - Project 4: Concrete Vessel

My general idea of the vessel was a cup that looked like a coil wraped around. I used clay to model out the shape of my vessel. I made a basic cup shape with the clay and rolled out strips that I wanted to wrap around the cup.

I then baked the cup and used it as the mold for the concrete vessel. To make a mold out of the cup, I had to make a container to pour the silicone. I made this container with acrylic and hot glue.

After testing the container for leaks, I placed the clay model in the container and poured the silicone solution. I used a kit I found to make the silicone It took about 30 minutes for the mold to dry. Once dried, I removed the silicone mold from the acrylic container. I then removed the model and the mold was ready to be used.

I used a ready to use concrete patch mix. I used this concrete because it was the first thing I found when I went to the store. I wasn't really sure if this was goint to work. After mixing the concrete, I poured it into the mold. Now I needed to wait for the concrete to set.

Overall this project helped me learn a lot of things about molding. It is a pretty simple idea, but it can help ypu make quality pieces very fast and efficiently.
